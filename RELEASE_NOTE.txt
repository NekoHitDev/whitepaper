Release v1.0.0
v1.0.0发布

This version matches the v1.0.0 of contract code.
In this version, we gave a detailed explanation of what is NekoHit Project,
what's the problem of the existing crowdfunding system, and how will we solve those
problems. We also defined the framework of the NekoHit Project, which is the
WCA contract, CAT token and NEKOIN token.
This will be the final shape of v1.X.X, all following updates shall not break the
compatibilities. For breaking changes, they will be included in v2 and become a
separate entity from v1.

该版本对应v1.0.0版本的合约代码。

解决这些问题。我们还定义了NekoHit Project的框架，即：WCA智能合约，CAT代币和NEKOIN代币。
这是v1.X.X系列的决定版本，之后的升级都不会破坏对该版本的兼容性。对于破坏性修订，他们将被
包含在v2版本中，成为一个独立于v1的版本定义。
