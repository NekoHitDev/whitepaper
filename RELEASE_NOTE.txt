Prerelease v0.0.1-RC2.
v0.0.1-RC2预发布

In this version we have further polished the white paper.
在这个预发布版本中我们进一步打磨了中英文白皮书的用词与句式。

English version is reviewed by Grammarly and should be more easy to read.
英文版经过Grammarly的指导与修改，应当更加容易阅读。

Chinese version is proofread based on the English version to maintain consistency.
中文版以英文版为参照进行校对，以保持内容的一致性。

Also, you're welcome to give suggestions about writing technics, or about this project.
另外，欢迎大家对写作技巧，或本项目相关事宜给出建议。

We are looking forward to your feedback.
我们很期待得到您的反馈。
